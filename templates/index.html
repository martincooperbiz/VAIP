What is VAIP?

The Virtual AI Patient project utilizes cutting-edge technologies, including AI and the ChatGPT LLM Model, to provide a training platform for medical and nursing students and professionals. With realistic patient simulations and AI-powered diagnostic assistance, it enhances diagnostic and communication skills, preparing them for real-world clinical scenarios.
